Input Format
None

Ouput Format
Rotation of pyramid along x-axis by 1 degree with 'x' key pressed on the keyboard.
Rotation of pyramid along y-axis by 1 degree with 'y' key pressed on the keyboard.
Rotation of pyramid along z-axis by 1 degree with 'z' key pressed on the keyboard.
Hold the key to see a view of continous 3-D Rotation.
