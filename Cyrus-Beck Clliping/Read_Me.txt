Input Format
Line 1: Two integers representing coordinates of lower left corner
Line 2: Two integers representing coordinates of upper right corner
Line 3: Single integer representing number of lines to be clipped.
Line 4: X and Y coordinates of each lines to be clipped.

Output Format
Clipped Lines and Clliping Window

Sample Input:
40 30
100 150
2
20 40
80 90
30 40
110 90
